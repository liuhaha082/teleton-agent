TON Blockchain Tools

15 tools for interacting with the TON blockchain: wallet management, TON transfers, jetton tokens, NFTs, and market data. Transfer tools are dm-only for security.

Wallet

ton_get_address

Get your TON wallet address (EQ… format).

ParameterTypeDescription
No parameters.

ton_get_balance

Check your current TON balance.

ParameterTypeDescription
No parameters.

ton_price

Fetch current TON/USD market price.

ParameterTypeDescription
No parameters.

ton_send dm-only

Transfer TON to an address. Restricted to DM context for security.

ParameterTypeDescription
addressstringDestination TON address (EQ… or UQ… format)
amountnumberAmount in TON (e.g. 1.5)
commentstring?Optional memo/comment attached to the transaction

Transactions

ton_get_transactions

Fetch transaction history for a specific TON address. For your own wallet, use ton_my_transactions.

ParameterTypeDescription
addressstringTON address (EQ… or UQ… format)
limitnumber?Max transactions (default: 10, max: 50)

ton_my_transactions

List your own recent transactions.

ParameterTypeDescription
limitnumber?Max transactions (default: 10, max: 50)

Market Data

ton_chart

Display price chart for TON or a jetton token.

ParameterTypeDescription
assetstring?Jetton address or ton (default: TON)
periodstring?Time period: 7d (default), 1d, 30d

dex_quote

Compare DEX swap quotes from STON.fi and DeDust side-by-side. Preview only, does not execute.

ParameterTypeDescription
from_assetstrington or jetton master address (EQ… format)
to_assetstrington or jetton master address (EQ… format)
amountnumberAmount in human-readable units (e.g. 10 for 10 TON)
slippagenumber?Slippage tolerance (0.01 = 1%, default: 0.01)

NFTs

nft_list

Browse NFTs owned by a wallet address.

ParameterTypeDescription
addressstring?Wallet address (defaults to your wallet)
limitnumber?Max NFTs to return (default: 50)

Jettons

jetton_send dm-only

Transfer jetton tokens to an address.

ParameterTypeDescription
toAddressstringDestination wallet address
jettonAddressstringJetton master contract address
amountnumberAmount in human-readable units
commentstring?Optional transaction comment

jetton_balances

List all jetton token balances in your wallet. Filters out blacklisted/scam tokens.

ParameterTypeDescription
No parameters.

jetton_info

Look up jetton contract metadata (name, symbol, decimals, supply).

ParameterTypeDescription
addressstringJetton master contract address

jetton_price

Fetch current spot price for a jetton.

ParameterTypeDescription
addressstringJetton master contract address

jetton_holders

List top holders of a jetton.

ParameterTypeDescription
addressstringJetton master contract address
limitnumber?Max holders (default: 100)

jetton_history

Fetch jetton market analytics and price history.

ParameterTypeDescription
addressstringJetton master contract address
periodstring?Time period: 7d (default), 1d, 30d
